Product configuration

Customized instead of standard.

Price calculation

Complex costs. Clearly structured.

Our dynamic calculation takes into account all individual parts, materials, and variants – including current data. This gives you an automatically generated, transparent price overview.

Quotation creation

One click. One complete document.

Technical and commercial data is automatically converted into a complete quotation as a Word file or PDF. No copy-paste errors, no loss of time.

Integrationsservice

Consulting on engineering tasks

Technical depth. Genuine consulting.

Heat exchangers in operation (digital twin)

Real-time transparency.

We digitally map the heat exchanger based on your data. Our web service provides you with an assessment of the condition or current performance of your heat exchanger – calculated individually based on your process data.

Company

We rely on modern, high-performance technology stacks in the Microsoft Azure Cloud, combined with proven system proximity through C++ and the flexibility of C#. Our solutions combine the efficiency of native applications with the scalability and security of modern cloud architectures.
By using Azure services such as Azure VMs, Databases, App Services, and Azure DevOps, we create robust, highly available applications—from system integration to complex backend services.

C++ is primarily used in performance-critical areas, while C# is our preferred language for cloud APIs, services, and automation. This results in future-proof, maintainable, and scalable solutions—tailored to the requirements of our customers.
